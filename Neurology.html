Neurology

The best care

The Department of Neurology has a team of highly trained specialists with vast experience in the field. They take pride in providing state of the art care with a patient-centric, empathetic approach in a variety of neurologic disorders ranging from migraine to movement Disorders. Having in-house facilities for advanced neurophysiological studies, 3-Tesla MRI, PET-CT and molecular diagnostics, the department offers comprehensive care to patients with acute stroke and cerebrovascular diseases, paediatric and adult epilepsy, neuromuscular diseases, Parkinson’s disease and related disorder, Alzheimer’s disease and other disorders of cognition, multiple sclerosis and other demyelinating disorders.
